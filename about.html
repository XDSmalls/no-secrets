Who I am

Ciao, I'm Samuele! I appreciate you visiting the site.

I spent more hours of my childhood watching other people cook (and drawing or playing videogames) than most people spend eating. I was the kid in the corner who kept asking questions nobody wanted to answer.

Why are you adding that now? How much of that is too much? What does "a pinch" actually mean to you, specifically?

The answers were always the same: "You'll know when it's ready." Or worse: "I don't know, I just do it."

I've spent a long time trying to figure out what they knew that they couldn't say, so this site is the result of that.
